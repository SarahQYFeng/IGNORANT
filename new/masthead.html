The digital world is a cup of Zombie
—please remain sober

Welcome to this edition, where we take a deep dive into the intersections of algorithms, technology, and humanity, examining the profound—and sometimes unexpected—ways these forces shape our world. As technology rapidly integrates into more aspects of our lives, the necessity to question, challenge, and understand it has never been more pressing. This issue brings together voices exploring the promises and perils of algorithms in our society.

Is the AI we interact with truly intelligent, or is it merely a reflection—a sometimes fuzzy, sometimes distorted—of the vast digital content we’ve created?

As algorithms increasingly mediate our daily choices, this piece asks what happens when we allow technology to inform our values, beliefs, and relationships. At what point do we begin to lose control over what it means to be human?

It’s a meditation on how technology, in trying to make sense of chaos, sometimes adds to it.

Our hope is that this issue fosters reflection, curiosity, and perhaps a bit of caution as we collectively venture further into this algorithmic age.
